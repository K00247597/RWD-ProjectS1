Underrated phones of 2020

By Jim hetry.

1/12/2020

Tt’s been a busy year for OnePlus, because as well as the OnePlus 8 range and the OnePlus 8T, the company has also launched a new line with the more affordable OnePlus Nord, and it might even be announcing the OnePlus Nord Lite soon, while in the first half of 2021 the company is sure to announce the OnePlus 9. As such, our list of the best OnePlus phones is regularly getting new additions, and will likely continue to do so for the foreseeable future. So it’s worth checking back here often if you want to stay up to date on them.

The most recent addition is the OnePlus 8T, which is an upgrade on the OnePlus 8 but not on the OnePlus 8 Pro – there’s no OnePlus 8T Pro, so the 8 Pro is still the flagship. There are plenty of other handsets to choose from as well, including the aforementioned OnePlus 8 Pro, and the older but still great OnePlus 7 Pro. So while there are always new OnePlus handsets on the way, there are already loads of great options to choose from – though note that with Black Friday and Cyber Monday both coming up, you might want to wait to buy any.
